By the time of the AI Practice launch in late 2025, Penta had already embedded AI into its workflow (e.g., using GPT-driven analysis to augment client research) and had governance measures in place (training programs, usage guardrails) ([pentagroup.com](https://pentagroup.com/insight/navigating-the-ai-revolution-five-essential-considerations-for-communication-pros#:~:text=3,about%20the%20dos%20and%20don%E2%80%99ts)). - Externally, Penta has translated its internal capabilities into a **clear client-facing value proposition** around AI. The Penta AI Practice offers distinct, relevant services (governance frameworks, reputation audits, synthetic testing) that address pressing client needs in the AI era ([pentagroup.com](https://pentagroup.com/insight/penta-launches-new-ai-practice-blending-stakeholder-insight-active-intelligence-and-applied-ai#:~:text=1)) ([pentagroup.com](https://pentagroup.com/insight/penta-launches-new-ai-practice-blending-stakeholder-insight-active-intelligence-and-applied-ai#:~:text=3)). The firm appears to be **ahead of most of its direct competitors** in blending data science into communications consulting (competitors might have AI initiatives, but Penta’s multi-year head start and depth of content is notable). There is every sign that Penta views AI as a transformative force for its industry, and it aims to be *“the indispensable guide”* for clients dealing with that transformation ([pentagroup.com](https://pentagroup.com/#:~:text=Your%20indispensable%20guide%20to%20an,uncertain%20world)). While it’s still early days for some of the outcomes (e.g., how much its AI efforts will drive growth or new client acquisitions), the institutional groundwork is in place. Penta’s posture can thus be summarized as: **All-in on AI, with a focus on practical stakeholder-focused applications, and rapidly evolving from early experimentation to a mature, integrative service offering.** The firm is moving beyond experimentation into execution, signaling a serious, sustained commitment to AI as a core pillar of its competitive edge in the advisory market.
