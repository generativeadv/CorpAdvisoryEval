It also subtly suggests Weber Shandwick’s offerings go beyond what an in-house team might do with basic AI tools – they will connect AI to business strategy.

In essence, Weber Shandwick’s positioning around AI is carefully balanced: **innovative and leading-edge, yet trustworthy and empathetic**.
